Delete Your Mobile Garage Account

We understand you may want to delete your account. To do so, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Mobile Garage app on your device.
  2. Navigate to Profile > Delete Account.
  3. Review the confirmation message and tap Confirm to delete your account permanently.

If you are unable to access the app or need help, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:

info@mg-profix.com

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days of your request.
